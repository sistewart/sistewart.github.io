Let's talk about the National Basketball Association!

This assignment is about one of my most favorite things in the world: the National Basketball Association. Basketball is my favorite sport (even though I can't play it). The National Basketball Association, the NBA, is one of the four major league sporting leagues in North America for men. It was formed in 1949 as a merger between two different organizations: the National Basketball League and the Basketball Association of America. Today, there are 29 teams in the U.S. and one team in Canada. The NBA is usuallly seen as the premier men's baksetball league in the world because basketball players come from all over the world come there to play.

The NBA Commissioner is the chief executive of the NBA. Here is a list of all the NBA Commissioners since the NBA was created.

NBA Commissioner Years
Maurice Podoloff 1949-1963
J. Walter Kennedy 1963-1975
Larry O'Brien 1975-1984
David Stern 1984-2014
Adam Silver 2014-Present

Five fun facts about the National Basketball Association

  • The three-point shot actually originiated in the Basketball Association of America, not the NBA.
  • The NBA Finals Trophy is named after former Commissioner Larry O'Brien.
  • The silhoutte in the NBA logo is actually LA Lakers legend, Jerry West.
  • Until the shot clock was introduced in the NBA, some players sat on the ball to keep the lead and make time run out.
  • There has been only one season that did not begin before New Year's Day. It was in 1999 due to a labor lockout.
