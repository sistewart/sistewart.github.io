LIVING HERE

Freshmen will find a place to call home right here on campus. We provide lots of options, from living and learning communities to more traditional residence hall settings.

Housing Communities >>
Our living and learning communities promote diversity and encourage interaction. Many have a specific academic or thematic focus, so you can immerse yourself in what you love.
Residence Halls >>
Get the scoop on our on-campus living options by comparing floor plans and taking a residence hall virtual tour. Find the hall that best suits you and get packing.
Apply for Housing >>
Once you've found the perfect place to live on campus, simply apply for housing online. University Housing will help you every step of the way.
Off-Campus Living >>
Freshmen are required to live on campus, but most of our graduate and transfer students are among the 70 percent of students who live off campus. Columbia is an easy-to-navigate city that offers plenty of sudent-centered housing. Visit Off-Campus Living & Neighborhood Relations to learn more about off-campus housing options, your rights as a tenant or to request legal services.

Great Value

With utilities included and the convenience of living so close to everything, on-campus housing is more affordable than you might think.
